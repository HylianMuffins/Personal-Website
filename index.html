All About Me

I was born in Calgary, AB in 2003 as the second oldest of 10 children. At the age of 11, I stumbled upon the JavaScript coding course on Khan Academy. This was my first introduction to code, and I knew I wanted to continue learning as much as I could.

In grade 9, I started attending St. John Choir Schola. I worked diligently throughout high school and graduated at the top of my class as Valedictorian.

In 2021, I was accepted into the Schulich School of Engineering at the UofC and have been pursuing a degree in Software Engineering ever since.

With a major in Software Engineering and a minor in Mechatronics Engineering, I plan on leading the world of tech and software forward.

I have always been impelled by the desire for quality. So when I imagine the future, I see a community of people working to raise the standard of quality in the industry, ultimately improving the quality of life for all who rely upon their contributions.
